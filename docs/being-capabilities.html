住民能力律The being capability fence — what each nature may hold, and what stays hidden

A capability a being cannot need, it should not hold. Every living thing in the world moves only by requesting — the host integrates and bounds the request — and the set of requests it may make is its fence. The default was to hand every being all eight, which is why a fisherman could fly: he held rise() because nothing had taken it away. This is the corrected law — least privilege, granted by nature.

凡能力，非其性所需者，不予。

A being holds only what its nature requires. The fisherman walks and boards his boat, so he keeps mv, bind, fr, other — but not rise, because a person does not fly. Enforcement is not a runtime check that can be forgotten: a person's grants simply omit rise, and a behavior that calls it dies at compile, before the being ever draws a frame. The unknown kind — one the AI invents — falls back to grounded: it may walk, sense and board, but flight is withheld until a kind is known to fly. The safe default for "I don't recognise this" is "it does not leave the ground".

Which to hide firstranked by reach × how wrong its misuse looks

If capabilities were revealed one nature at a time, this is the order in which they should be withheld — most-hidden first. It is the order in which their misuse is most visible and least appropriate.

  1. rise — flight

    Hide from everything but flyers. This is the one that sent Galen's fisherman out through the top of the world and out of sight. Its misuse is the most visible failure there is — a being simply disappears. Granted only to birds and butterflies; a person, a fish, a boat can never hold it.

  2. bind · unbind — riding

    Hide from what does not board. Riding attaches one being to another and lets it inherit a carrier's motion; misused it builds strange attachment graphs (a temple riding a fish). Kept for people who step into boats; withheld from swimmers, flora and structures.

  3. mv — walking

    Hide from the rooted and the inert. A temple that could mv would walk off its foundations; a maple would stroll. Their motion, where they have any, is skin animation or host physics — never a step the cell requests.

  4. fr · other — sensing

    Grant where a being acts on what it senses; withhold from pure scenery. A schooling fish needs other to follow its neighbours; a swaying reed needs neither. Sensing carries no reach on its own, so it is hidden last among the world-touching capabilities — but décor that only sways is given none.

  5. get · set — private state

    Never hidden. Thirty-two private slots of scratch memory: an animation phase, a timer, a remembered target. They touch nothing outside the being, so they carry no reach and belong to every nature, down to the stillest stone.
